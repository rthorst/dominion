[edit] Card Lists

There have been seven card rankings so far - one each year starting in 2011. (Previously the 2011 rankings were published as "2012 Ed. 1", but they were really the 2011 poll results.) Starting in 2017, Qvist distributed the poll stats to a number of other members who published the results in multiple posts per costs from $2-$5. If you want to find the link to the rank results for a particular cost from $2-$5, it is best to just follow the overall 2017 link below, and look for the list of all 2017 ranking links at the bottom of the first post.

  • The 1st, 2011 Ed., had 30 users submit rankings.
  • The 2nd, 2012 Ed., had 65 users submit rankings.
  • The 3rd, 2013 Ed., had 55 users submit rankings. Addition of Dark Ages - new categories for Ruins, Knights, & Prizes
  • The 4th, 2014 Ed., had ~130 users submit rankings. Addition of Guilds
  • The 5th, 2015 Ed., had ~100 users submit rankings. Addition of Adventures - Events included in regular rankings by card cost and additionally as a separate list
  • The 6th, 2016 Ed., had ~65 users submit rankings. Addition of Empires - new category for Landmarks
  • The 7th, 2017 Ed., had ~45 users submit rankings. Addition of Nocturne - new categories for Boons & Hexes, removed cards no longer ranked
  • The 8th, 2018 Ed., had ~40 users submit rankings. (30-35 votes for each card in the categories by cost, with fewer votes in the other categories, and only ~13 votes for landmarks, boons and hexes.) Addition of Renaissance - Projects included in regular rankings by card cost and additionally as a separate list

Users submitted their lists in each category to Qvist who took the average and other statistics on the ranking of each card. The averages were weighted by the users' levels on Dominion Online (isotropic in the beginning, now ShuffleIT) in order to bias ratings slightly towards those from more experienced players. The actual list was determined by sorting the averages of each card's rankings. To make the lists comparable independent of the number of cards in a specific list and to make it possible to take part even though not knowing all expansions, the 2013 Edition's list were published with percentage values. The 2013 Edition also first introduced cards lists from non-kingdom cards like Ruins, Knights and Prizes. Below the lists of each category are shown. +/- shows the change in comparison to last year. It ignores the addition of new cards (like the Dark Ages cards in 2013). Cards in italics have been removed from the second editions of Base and Intrigue.
